Have fun at SE club!

Software Engineering (SE) Club is a committee that works to make the student experience
and the software engineering programs at Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology,
University of Malaya the best it can be! The committee regularly plan events that allow software
engineering students to network with the fellow students, alumni and industries. Additionally,
professional development events are organised to help students to gain a better understanding of the
SE field:

  • Talks by industry professionals and SE experts
  • Workshops
  • Competitions
  • SE Day
  • Gathering with alumni
